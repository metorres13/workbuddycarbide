Taper Per Foot Explained

A 3° taper per side sounds small, but over 200 mm of length it produces a diameter change of over 10 mm. On a Haas lathe, setting the compound rest to the correct angle is a common source of scrap. This calculator simplifies the process so you can focus on making parts, not doing manual trig on the shop floor.

Converting Taper to Lathe Compound Angle

Whether you work on a Haas VF-2, a Mazak Integrex, or an Okuma MB-5000, understanding this calculation is essential for consistent quality. Many CAM systems handle it automatically, but knowing how to verify the output prevents costly programming errors.

Frequently Asked Questions
